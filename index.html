Welcome

A gathering place for service, agriculture, and friendship.

For generations, Granges across America have brought rural families together to support agriculture, education, civic engagement, and community life. Lewis Grange #406 carries that spirit forward with events, meals, fundraisers, music, and neighborly support.

Our hall is located at 20645 Road W, Lewis, CO 81327.
